Google just launched App Engine, a new storage and application service, free for now, (pay a premium later if you need bandwidth or storage ).


It's covered everywhere, but the highlights are: BigTable as DB , Python and supports Python libraries , dJango and compatible frameworks. All Python and BigTable, no C, no SQL.

Direct hit for Bungee and Amazon, and a good and cheap way to fund startup projects. If you have a lot of hits they might buy your app :-)

The more interesting parts from an IT standpoint are
  1. infrastructure and hosting as commodity.
  2. Non relational DBMSes gaining ground for cloud-based apps , web 2.0 and document apps.
