All the big players are prepping their ammo for what it appears to be the next battle for the end-users desktops.

We have Google with Desktop search, Sidebar, Picasa/Hello, Gmail , Blogger, and Talk ( more coming).

Also Yahoo! with Yahoo desktop search, Konfabulator, Flickr, Yahoo Mail, 360, and Yahoo messenger ( recently added voice)

And in this corner, the former champion, Microsoft, Microsoft Desktop search, MSN Spaces, Hotmail, Messenger ( now with a corporate version, and also a voice upgrade in the works, since they purchased a smal VOIP firm).

Our redmond's friends have launched a beta of a Picasa/Hello like client based on WinFX codenamed Max !, and thare is a lot of buzz around MS Gadgets wich are a little too much like konfabulator/expose if you ask me , but actually they are Vista's sidebar panels, and will appear in other places as well

I find really interesting to watch these developments, but , how cool it would be ( never will happen, but ... to dream is free...) if you can interop say flickr with MN Spaces/messenger, and GMail and Google desktop search... :-).
