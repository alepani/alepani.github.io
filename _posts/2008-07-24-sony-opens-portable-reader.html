--- layout: post title: Sony opens portable reader date: '2008-07-24T13:39:00.002-03:00' author: Alejandro tags: - sony - books - gadgets - media - ebooks modified_time: '2008-07-24T13:56:43.494-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3848561.post-6144445404227841911 blogger_orig_url: http://alepanizza.blogspot.com/2008/07/sony-opens-portable-reader.html --- In a recent news release Sony (SNE) announced that the PRS-505 portable reader will be getting a firmware patch that will allow the device to read .epub and .pub files ( same used for adobe ebook reader ) included in de Open Publication Standard and backed by open by the IDPF.

This will let Sony get a lot of new books from different vendors and stores, and will hurt the kindle wich has currently more selection and relies on book sales to profit from the device.
Sony will keep the book store , but my guess is they are gaining money from the hardware already , and do not want another "Playstation business" with the books.

I've had the reader for about a year now , and frankly I'm quite happy , book prices are on par with the kindle and the selection is ok, the only weak point is technical books (programming, science, math), wich are almost non-existant maybe this move will get more content and better prices , good news.
