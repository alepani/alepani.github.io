Amazon at retail found such a model in reviews and reccomendation from a large and loyal community, iTunes/iPod instant gratification and convenience got the music industry ( a billion dollar business , that went on for half a century) in the hands of a small computer company in california. A programmer (Cohen) with a good idea for network distribution (Bittorrent) is undermining Movies and TV giants.

A lot of small and medium cable and media companies are actually scared about all this ( and they should be) , either they find a distributor and become content providers, or get out of the way. Statistical data show that TV watching and Movie theater sales decline in direct proportion with broadband usage.

There are lots of startups that tried to get into the internet video distribution, YouTube for the casual user, iTunes for small independent video podcast and Network TV series, and the latest one : Joost .
