--- layout: post title: Apple and Intel ? date: '2005-06-06T23:53:00.001-03:00' author: Alejandro tags: modified_time: '2005-06-07T00:47:46.340-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3848561.post-111811437476593433 blogger_orig_url: http://alepanizza.blogspot.com/2005/06/apple-and-intel.html --- Big news today, as expected, Steve Jobs announced that Apple is "switching" :-) over to Intel chips. The main reason is getting higher perfomance (3Ghz and up) , and cooler processors, for their new Powerbooks, something that IBM's PowerPC chips had failed to deliver.

This was reported first in CNet( I think) and blogged everywhere.
I like Om's take on the issue . More here

What is more surprising to me, though is that:

a)They had an up-to-date x86 build of Mac OSX running for the last five years "just in case" , (I could have used that )

b) Wolfram Research's Mathematica ( this is very complex software if you hadn't used it), was ported in just 2 hours.

Will this mean that you could be able to run OsX in any Intel based PC ? Absolutely not. Apple business is 50% hardware .
Will you be able tu run windows in a future Apple x86 based PC , I hope so. That would be neat.
