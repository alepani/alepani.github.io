--- layout: post title: GeneXus XV User's meeting date: '2005-09-13T22:53:00.000-03:00' author: Alejandro tags: modified_time: '2005-09-16T01:25:38.906-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3848561.post-112666458366653775 blogger_orig_url: http://alepanizza.blogspot.com/2005/09/genexus-xv-users-meeting.html --- Our company, GeneXus host an annual user's meeting/expo since 1989, ( we skipped a year because we moved from october to march/april, to september again in 2001/2002).

This is a technical/business event, with almost 3,000 people attending, mostly from South America, but also from the US, Mexico, Italy, Spain, China, Japan...
We get together to discuss the future versions, to keep in touch with the latest developments/technics and to establish business opportunities.

On these three days, there are over a hundred conferences, keynotes, and developer's rountables. We usually have a good time, and get to know wat the users are up to, and what they are expecting from us.

The conferences are mainly technical, but not all are centered around the product, there are also general interest conferences, off-topic ones, and big players (Microsoft , IBM, Oracle, Sun ...) have their space to.
