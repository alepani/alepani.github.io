They are not cheap, but when you think that you're also paying for reliability ( I mean , how many times the Amazon Store went down in the past 5 years ? ) it does not sound so bad.

Now good old Jeff Bezos is adding more icing to the cake, and I 'm beggining to think he has a master plan after all, and that google should be worrying about AMZN , not yahoo , or Microsoft....

Amazon is starting a Beta of FPS ( not not a shooter :-) ) but Flexible Payment Service, a service designed for developers to adopt the amazon payment system, as Kyle puts it this could make total sense, you have an e-commerce site, the last thing you want to go bad is payments and if you're using Amazon's service you already have millions ( no kidding) of customers and their pre-approved credit cards in your database. Including mine and probably yours as well.
