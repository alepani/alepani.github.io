There where major announcement this week in the WWOG ( Wonderful World of Gadgets) .

In no particular order:

Nintendo announced a price ($249 Wii Sports included) and a date (November 19th) for the Wii console launch, with various games available at launch, including Zelda.
A lot of announcement details, virtual console, wii "channels", etc

Apple. Updated it's entire ipod line, making the shuffle smaller, doubling the nano capacity; changing the colours and, cutting also the regular ipod prices as well as pre-announcing both , a movie download service (now with disney movies only) and a set top box (iTV) to stream the movies from itunes into your TV.

Microsoft, not to be left behind is preparing it's own "Ipod killer" the zune sporting 30 GB, FM , WiFi, video, and a rumored 280 price (in line with apple offers) seems like a good player. A Lot of details at engadget blog
