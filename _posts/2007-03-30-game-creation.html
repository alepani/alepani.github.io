I have been very interested in how to teach kids to program lately ; and a friend sent me a link (via geekdad ) to a software that combines teaching programming with making games: called Game Factory 2.

This "game" lets kids make games and teaches them the basic of modern programming: events, conditions and objects, it's called game factory 2.

My kids ( 6 and 9 ) love it, and are now making their first "levels" of their own.

It's very simple but if your kid is inclined to drawing and design this will grab his/her attention and will prepare the way for him to more advanced logic and programming enviroments. It's also a great and fun way to spend time playing with the family.
