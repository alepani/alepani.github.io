They trashed Mozilla, and got kicked by It's leaner meaner reincarnation: Firefox.

App Engine

App Engine is Googles "Infrastructure as a service" or "Cloud Based platform"

Again they are late to the party behind Amazon, and others.

The google AppEngine is a Python based aplication repository, with BigTable as the database.

The competitors for AppEngine have a lot of value added, Amazon, provides full blown virtual machines , your pick of DBMS, (S3 , mySQL or whatever) and Language (anything you can run in the machine), and they have the maturity to charge for the staff and doing fine.

Facebook also have their online platform, and beside providing a robst infrastrucure, no cost development, they are bringing a social net of 100 million of potential users ...

Microsoft is working on something , probably C#/Silverlight based dso they have another guy to worry about.


Finally

The point of all this is (or was) I've lost the path of the argument... :-)
is that Google is making insane amounts of money , but, they have one (1) profitable business, search based ads.

They have at least half a dozen big important projects ( Android, Mail, Google Apps, Appengine, Health, The Browser, Desktop Search, Blogger , Picasa, etc) that don't make any money and are not even close to being a full featured product , and in some cases are way behind what's currently available.

Is Search the only thing Google can do ? Certaily not, they are too smart for that , but they have to take the expansion thing Microsoft-seriously or sooner or later they will be a commodity.
