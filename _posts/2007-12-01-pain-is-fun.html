Downstairs I hear screaming, and then the sound of something hitting metal, " It hurts! " ( one of my kids ) , " Quick grab the old lady" , a while later " Let's throw him through the glass" ( the little one) , " watch the wall !", then a loud sound of something breaking and then, laughing...



No , I'm not going crazy :-) , my two older kids are playing PAIN, which came up on the PSN this week. Basically it's a "sandbox" physics based game, where you throw your character from a giant slingshot in order to hit him against several targets ( walls , windows, cranes, signs, vehicles, and also monkeys, mimes and yes , old ladies....)

The game is $10, a bit expensive for what is is , but for me it's worth it, although a bit limited , it's a lot of fun. :-)
