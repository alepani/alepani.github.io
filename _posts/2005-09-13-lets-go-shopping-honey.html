eBay just bought Skype for the *incredibly ridiculous* price of 1.3 billion dollars and 38 million shares ( at the current ask price for eBay that's another 1 billion additional and some other change) plus another 1.5 b. IF certain conditions are met .... 4.1 billion total. I'm sure they have their reasons, but Om is going nuts about this and I just don't know how they got so much out of the deal.
I'm actually sad this happened, because I fear skype will share the destiny of ICQ and hotmail and get absrbed into the big service and lose it's appeal as a high quality independent voice client... moving to Google Talk..

In related news, one of the old school :-) CRM software giants, Siebel Systems, got his price also, when sold to Oracle yesterday for ( get ready for this..) 5.3 Billion. Maybe somebody is expecting to challenge MS and SAP, or just beating a dead horse....?
