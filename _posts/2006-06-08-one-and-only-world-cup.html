The football World Cup ( that's soccer for the US).

The host country Germany has a easy match with Costa Rica.

There are few sports that draw this level of universal attention and fanatism, the world productivity declines, companies and countries sets meetings and schedules around the matches, piles of money are bet ( legally and illegally) around the world, and people from bolivia , france, japan and arabia and africa, tune in the TV at the same time to watch "the beautiful game".

The US mainstream press ( and most bloggers) still struggle to make sense of the universal appeal of "soccer" with various degrees of success, but mostly they are still at odds with the most popular sport in the world.
