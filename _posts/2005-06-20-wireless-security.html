I 've brought a Pocket PC from work (it has WiFi built in), and walking around my neighborhood, I've found at least two Access Points with no security at all.
So you should use WEP encryption right ? Wrong
as this flash movie proves, anybody can crack WEP security in 10 minutes, it's like crackin for dummies...
So what's a family man to do ?
I need my wife's notebook and mine's connected, and I have 802.11b and .11g cards, so while WPA /AES based security would be nice, it's not an option.
MAC filtering works fine for me so i only allow this two particular cards to connect to the network, and I still use security to prevent sniffing, just in case
