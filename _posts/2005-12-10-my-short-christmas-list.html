This are the things I think will make the best gift ( most of them are geek stuff , so it depends on what your tastes are and who is the gift-receiver)

Books: The book to read this year is Steven Levy's Freakonomics this is a general audience book, but the authors have a deep understanding of the microeconomics of everyday things, and a great writing style.

Gadgets: If you are looking for a digital camera the Sony DSC-T7 sure has the looks ...but if you're seriously into this , maybe the canon digital rebel XT is best for you, I had a friend who has one and it's an amazing camera.

The IPod Nano is the best way to listen to music, period. My wife has one and loves it.

For Kids The Nintendo DS has a great games it's less than 150 and lets you play gams online all around the world.

Speaking of games any PS2 Ratchet game wuld be an excellent gift or if you hava a GameCube Mario Kart Double Dash

For grown-ups I'll recommend Shadow of the collossus (PS2) and Resident Evil 4 (GC and PS2)
both very good games.



Merry Xmas!
