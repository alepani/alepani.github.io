--- layout: post title: The soul of a new machine ( a Pentium M) date: '2005-11-23T00:56:00.000-02:00' author: Alejandro tags: modified_time: '2005-11-24T18:13:41.160-02:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3848561.post-113271655636526384 blogger_orig_url: http://alepanizza.blogspot.com/2005/11/soul-of-new-machine-pentium-m.html --- My old laptop was a toshiba satellite S5205 series, it was a fairly good laptop, with a pentium desktop 4, a high resolution ( 1400x1200) screen , and a great pair of HK speakers.

Unfortunately it died a horrible dead in a freaky accident involving a nylon case and a bottle of coke... I'm not going into much detail here :-) , except to say that the repair costs were more than a new low end machine.

So.. I decided to go for a new one, I wasn't planning to buy a new laptop until next year , but I needed one, so I went shopping with what little cash I had.. The HPs , compaqs, averatecs, alienwares, and Dells and Sonys were considered, and discarded, I like sony's design, and Dell quality but I ended up buying toshiba again.

The final decision was in favor of a satellite M45. I wanted a 15 inch ( not more) and a Pentium M ( I'm done with desktop processors )

Four main reasons:

  • Design, it's not as sleek as a sony but it's nicer looking than any other box around at the price
  • Firewire ( the only one offering Firewire ports standard in low end models)
  • Price : it costs $1400 for a Pentium M/ 1 GB / 100G hd /wxga trubrite machine almost 500 less than a similar configured competitor..
  • Double layer DVD writer / Multiformat card reader/ S-Video Out/ wifi all built in

Pros: The machine is fast, it has a massive hd, good wifi range, and it's lighter than the one I had also , it has a DVD burner that can record on almost any format.

Cons: Good speakers , but not near as good as the 5205, lower resolution screen, trackpad was better in previous versions, ( no scroll wheel in trackpad), No external battery level indicator.

Overall I'm satisfied, but not thrilled with the machine, It's clearly the result of a mix of technology advancement, well thought features and cost cutting. But it's a budget machine and at 1400 It's a great deal.
