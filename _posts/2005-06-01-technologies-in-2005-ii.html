Podcasting: Seems like a good idea, but it has (yet) no clear advantage over downloading an mp3 using something like bittorrent , the software lacks polishing and the content is not there.

Home media receivers: Current offers lack easy of use or quality or both. Wifi connections are not fast enogh to transmit video content, some wireld models are ok, but the software is still not ready, Media Center PCs are too expensive, and too noisy . I'm sticking with my DVD player for now.
Eventually the concept will work, having all your media in one machine makes sense, but I guess we'lll have to wait for something more consumer friendly to do the trick.
