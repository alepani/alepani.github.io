I am a regular user of del.icio.us tags, I even use it with several accounts, for different projects, and have a personal account for my personal interest links.
I remember in the "bubble days" ( 1998ish ) there were a lot of this type of services ( bookmarks online), but without the "social" part , I really like to be able to see what's popular, and what other people I know, have linked to. Another example of this is digg.com something similar but more news oriented.
That said, the del.icio.us interface sucks :-), luckily somebody made an ajax front end callled direc.tor, it's not perfect, but I can live with it.
