--- layout: post title: A killer app for mobile date: '2008-09-02T23:47:00.004-03:00' author: Alejandro tags: modified_time: '2008-09-30T21:36:33.194-03:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-3848561.post-5708449273067534413 blogger_orig_url: http://alepanizza.blogspot.com/2008/09/killer-app-for-mobile.html --- If you have already visited the Apple AppStore, you may have noticed that most software there falls into three categories:
  • Free extensions of online business ( Facebook, Ebay)
  • Cute little games and utilities ( Calories counters, Measuring and Logging, Monkey Ball, etc)
  • And garbage that you can't pay for.
The fact that most Aplications are useless, is a sign of the limited toolset ( Mac only) and the first wave of applications, it will get better and hopefuly the killer app will show up, eventually.

Facebook is the best example of the free apps, I haven't found an application worth paying for...
and I'm not sure if an "enterprise" app would work on an IPhone. As a personal device is truly brilliant.

One of my favorite contenders is Qik ( qik.com ) ( not in the appstore, you need an "open" iphone) , but it is also available also for Symbian S60 based 3G phones (most new Nokias, and some Motorola and Samsung devices)

Qik is live streaming of Video via 3G or Wifi cameraphones and it works increadibly well, the support is extremely good and works everywhere ( 3g or wifi).
